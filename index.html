About the Group

A Group Built on Specialisation, Not Diversification

Shri Karni Group of Companies was founded with a clear philosophy — focus deeply on fewer domains rather than spread thin across many. This is reflected in two dedicated entities, each with its own certifications, equipment, and expertise.

From airport electrical systems to runway civil works, both entities operate independently while sharing the group's core values of quality, safety, and timely delivery.
