Our Services

Every Phase of Airport Civil Works

Comprehensive civil contracting across the full airside and landside scope of airport infrastructure.

Runway Construction & Resurfacing

Full-depth reclamation, flexible and rigid pavement overlays, shoulder widening, runway marking, and lighting trench works — executed within aircraft operations windows.

Terminal Civil Works

Structural civil works for passenger terminals — ground slabs, drainage, utility trenching, exterior paving, and finishing to CPWD and AAI standards.

Taxiway & Apron Construction

High-load concrete and bituminous aprons and taxiways designed for wide-body aircraft, with precision PCN compliance and drainage integration.

Airside Drainage & Utilities

Storm water drainage networks, box culverts, catch basins, fuel hydrant civil works, and underground utility laying including trenchless technology.

Perimeter Fencing & Security Roads

Perimeter fencing, internal security roads, access control foundation works, and watchtower construction to BCAS and AAI specifications.

Ancillary Airport Buildings

Civil works for ATC towers, equipment rooms, cargo sheds, fire stations, and other ancillary airport facilities under turnkey or package contracts.

How We Work

From Tender to Handover

A structured delivery process refined across every airport project we have completed.

1

Tender Analysis

BOQ review, site assessment, and compliance checks before bidding — no surprises during execution.

2

Site Mobilisation

Own equipment fleet mobilises within days. No critical dependency on external machinery hire.

3

Works Execution

Night-window and shutdown execution coordinated with ATC and airport operations in real time.

4

QA & Testing

In-house lab testing for CBR, compaction, and pavement quality per IRC and AAI specifications.

5

Handover & DLP

Structured handover with as-built drawings, QA records, and full defect liability period support.
