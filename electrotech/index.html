Our Services

Electrical Works — From Grid to Socket

A complete range of electrical contracting services, each executed by certified engineers and technicians.

HT / LT Power Distribution

High and low tension cable laying, jointing, and termination for industrial plants, commercial complexes, and government facilities. Includes overhead line works and underground cabling.

Substation Erection & Commissioning

Design, supply, erection, and commissioning of HT/LT substations including transformer installation, switchgear panels, bus bars, and protection systems.

Airport Electrical Systems

Airfield ground lighting (AGL), constant current regulators (CCR), approach lighting, runway edge lights, taxiway lighting, and power supply systems compliant with DGCA and AAI standards.

Industrial & Commercial Wiring

Internal wiring, panel boards, DB installation, lighting systems, and power circuits for factories, warehouses, hospitals, and commercial buildings.

Earthing & Lightning Protection

Conventional and chemical earthing systems, lightning arrestors, surge protection devices, and earth mat design for substations and industrial facilities.

Energy Audit & Power Quality

Electrical energy audits, power factor correction, harmonic analysis, and load balancing studies for industrial and commercial clients to reduce energy costs and improve efficiency.
